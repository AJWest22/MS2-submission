Assassins v. Templars

Instructions:

Scroll down the page to the yellow square, this contains your armies, click and drag your squads to the green grid on the left, you can rotate your armies if you want to, when you have finished placing them, click start game, and left click a square in the templar's grid on the right. The game ends when you have either hit all the Templar's armies, or hit the templar's captain. Note: This game is not yet playable on mobile or tablet, though we have plans to make it mobile friendly in the near future.

Assassins v. Templars

Instructions:

Wlecome to Assassin's v. Templars, a battleship inspired take on the popular video game franchise, Assassin's Creed. The objective of this game is to find and hit all the Templar's armies. The squads to hit are: Lookout Post (x2), Supplies (x3), Footsoldiers (x3), Knights (x4), Generals (x5). You have the same squads. Click and drag your squads in the purple square to the blue grid on the left, you can rotate your armies if you want to, when you have finished placing them, click start game, and left click a square in the templar's purple grid on the right. The game ends when you have either hit all the Templar's armies. Note: This game is not yet playable on mobile or tablet, though we have plans to make it mobile friendly in the near future.
